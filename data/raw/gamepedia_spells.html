Spell

From Hearthstone: Heroes of Warcraft Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Spell cards are cards that can be played to trigger a one time effect or ability, described in the card's text. Spell cards do not have an Attack or Health value and can only be used during your turn. Spell cards are the second most common type of card.

Notes[edit | edit source]

In Hearthstone the term spell is not limited to describing magical effects, but is roughly equivalent to the term ability from games like World of Warcraft, including magical abilities, traditional spell-casting, and physical abilities such as Cleave, Eviscerate and Slam. Spell cards generally represent all actions taken directly by a hero, aside from combat and Hero Powers.

Spell cards[edit | edit source]

All collectible spells are class-specific. Spells in the "Any" class category are either uncollectible cards granted to players through neutral cards such as Elite Tauren Chieftain, or are debug or missions cards that cannot be obtained by players.
